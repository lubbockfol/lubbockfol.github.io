Friends of the Library
Mission Statement

The purpose of FOL shall be:

To maintain an association of persons interested in the Library;

To focus attention on the Library and promote its use in the community;

To receive and encourage gifts, endowments and bequests to the Library;

To support and cooperate with the Library in developing library services and facilities for the community;

To create an awareness of the services offered by the Lubbock public Library System;

And to promote reading in the community.

FOL received its Charter
June 12, 1967
