50th Anniversary Celebration

The Friends of the Lubbock Library celebrates 50 years of service to the Lubbock Library with a series of special events in 2017. The festivities culminate Oct. 6-8, 2017 with a special appearance by Jessica Fellowes, author of Downton Abbey: A Celebration.

Niece of Downton Abbey creator and screenwriter Julian Fellowes, Ms. Fellowes has authored five companion guides to the award-winning TV series. Her latest installment, Downton Abbey: A Celebration, features interviews, photographs, and a complete episode guide covering all six seasons of the hit show. She has also authored several non-fiction books. View her complete works on her website, https://jessicafellowes.wordpress.com/books/

Events include:

Downton Abbey screening
September 21, 7pm, at the Cactus Theater
FOL and KTTZ are co-hosting a special screening of Downton Abbey. General admission tickets are free but must be reserved in advance; ticket reservations will begin in August.

Jessica Fellowes talk for students
October 6, 3pm, at the Cactus Theater

FOL 50th Anniversary Gala
October 7 at the Lubbock Women’s Club, 2020 Broadway; door opens 6pm, program starts 7pm
Ms. Fellows will be the guest of honor at the FOL 50th Anniversary Gala. A limited number of table sponsorships are still available to local organizations and individuals.

Jessica Fellowes literary talk
October 8, 3:00 PM, City Bank Auditorium
Ms. Fellowes will present Real Life Inspiration for Downton Abbey at a literary talk hosted by the Friends of the Library. Tickets sales will begin in August. VIP seating includes a pre-event meet and greet with the author.

Friends of the Lubbock Public Library Day – 50 Years of Service
October 8

Mayor Dan Pope and the Lubbock City Council will proclaim October 8, 2017, Friends of the Lubbock Public Library Day! Here is the text of the proclamation:

Whereas: In June 1967 the Friends of the Lubbock Public Library (FOL) incorporated as a charitable organization for the benefit of the Lubbock Public Library system (Library), and

Whereas: Over 1500 current FOL members and over 150 current FOL volunteers dedicate over 25,000 hours annually for this purpose, and

Whereas: The trust of the citizens of Lubbock and surrounding area has been placed in the FOL to administer donations of books and funds to support programs of the Library, and

Whereas: The FOL has faithfully administered these donations providing funds for numerous youth and adult reading programs, literacy, and other assistance as may arise in the operation of the Library, and

Whereas: The City of Lubbock and its citizens wish to gratefully recognize this dedicated service to the Library and Lubbock Community,

Therefore: We proclaim October 8, 2017, as Friends of the Lubbock Public Library Day – 50 Years of Service.
