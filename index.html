The Friends of the Lubbock Public Library, Inc. (FOL), was incorporated in 1967. We are a non-profit organization that raises funds in support of the Lubbock Public Library System. FOL provides financial support and literary programs for community members of all ages.

View our year-round online book sales on eBay and AbeBooks.

FOL is run entirely by volunteers! We need your help to make all our activities a success.
  • Sort donations
  • Lead and organize book sections
  • Assist at book sales
  • And more! (See our Volunteer page.)

All volunteers must be active FOL members. Join the FOL and sign up today!
