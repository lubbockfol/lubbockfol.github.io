Membership

Members wanted! Memberships are available to all book enthusiasts.

How to join: Sign up and pay your annual membership dues at any regular book sale, or Saturday bookstore, or print out the form below and mail it or turn it in at any Lubbock Public Library branch, with your payment.

Membership Form (Adobe PDF)

Become a member of FOL and enjoy all these benefits:

  • Shopping every Saturday at the FOL Bookstore at the Mahon Library, 10am-2pm. (Not all areas of the sales floor are available.)

  • Early shopping at our book sales

  • Subscription to The Bookmark, our FOL newsletter

  • Knowledge that your support directly contributes to library book purchases and literary programs for our community

Choose the membership level that fits you:

Student: $5
Family or individual: $10
Bronze Patron: $25
Silver Patron: $50
Gold Patron: $75
Platinum Patron: $100

Friends of the Lubbock Library is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization. Your annual dues and all purchases are tax-deductible.
